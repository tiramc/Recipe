Chocolate Quinoa Pudding

Ingredient Amount
Cooked, cooled quinoa 1 cup
Almond milk 3/4 to 1/4 cup
Maple syrup 2 tablespoons
Cacao Powder 3 tablespoons
Packed dates 1/3 cup
Vanilla Extract 1 teaspoon
Salt One pinch
  1. Place all ingredients (starting with just 3/4 cup almond milk) in a blender and pur√©e until completely smooth. Add additional almond milk as needed to reach desired consistency (it will firm up a bit in the fridge though!)
  2. Pour into a bowl or container and cover; set in refrigerator until chilled. Serve.

Notes

How moist/dry your quinoa will dictate how much almond milk you need for this recipe. Mine was leftover in the fridge so it was pretty dry- I used 1 full cup. You can also use a ripe banana instead of dates to sweeten and flavor the pudding.
